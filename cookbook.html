  1. Cereal

Cereal

Ingredients:
  • Skim Milk
  • Cereal
Recipe:
  1. Pour desired amount of cereal from bag into bowl.
  2. Pour milk until cereal is covered. All surface area of every piece of cereal must have contacted the milk.
  3. Set for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Enjoy!
